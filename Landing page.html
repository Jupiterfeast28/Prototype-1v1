How do I upload my resume?

Sign in, go to your profile, and select 'Upload Resume.' Supported formats: PDF and DOCX. Your resume will be parsed for skills, experience, and education. Edit parsed details as needed before saving.

What job filters can I use?

Filter jobs by location, work type (remote, hybrid, on-site), salary range, seniority, and tags. Use the search and filter tools on the job search page to narrow your results.

How can I track my applications?

View your application status in your dashboard. Each application displays its current stage, such as 'Submitted,' 'Phone Screen,' or 'Offer Pending.' Updates are provided as your application progresses.

How is my data kept secure?

All data is protected with encryption, secure password storage, GDPR-compliant handling, and malware scanning for uploaded files. Privacy settings default to private until you opt in.
