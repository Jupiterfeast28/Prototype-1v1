Help Center

Find answers to common questions about using PathAI.

For Candidates

How do I upload and parse my resume?

Go to your profile and click "Upload Resume." Our AI will extract your skills and experience. You can edit all information before saving.

What file formats are supported?

We accept PDF and DOCX formats, up to 10MB.

How do I search for jobs?

Use our job search page to filter by location, salary, work type, and skills.

How can I track my applications?

Visit your "Applications" dashboard to see the status of every job application.

For Employers

How do I post a job?

Click "Post a Job" and fill in the job details. Your job will be live immediately.

How does the hiring pipeline work?

Candidates move through stages: Applied, Under Review, Interview, Offer, and Hired. Add notes and feedback at each stage.

Can I move candidates between stages?

Yes. Click any candidate card and use the action buttons to move them forward.

How is data kept secure?

All data is encrypted and GDPR compliant with industry-standard malware scanning.
